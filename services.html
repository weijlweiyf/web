Our Services

At China Shopping Helper, we provide a variety of services designed to make your shopping experience in China as smooth and efficient as possible. Whether you're looking to source products for your business or simply want to find the best deals, we've got you covered.

Product Search and Comparison

We offer an easy-to-use search tool that allows you to find products across major Chinese e-commerce platforms. Compare prices, read reviews, and make informed decisions with our comprehensive search functionality.

Translation Assistance

Understanding product descriptions and specifications can be challenging when dealing with a foreign language. Our translation tools help you translate product names and descriptions from English to Chinese and vice versa, ensuring you know exactly what you're buying.

Shopping Guides and Tips

New to shopping on Chinese e-commerce sites? Our detailed guides and tips will help you navigate platforms like 1688, Pinduoduo, Taobao, and Yiwu Shopping. Learn about the best practices for finding reliable suppliers, securing the best prices, and avoiding common pitfalls.

Custom Sourcing Service

Need help finding specific products or suppliers? Our custom sourcing service connects you with verified suppliers who meet your requirements. We handle the communication, negotiation, and logistics to ensure you get the best deal.

Order Management

Managing orders from multiple suppliers can be overwhelming. Our order management service consolidates your orders, provides regular updates, and ensures timely delivery. Focus on your business while we handle the details.

Quality Control and Inspection

Worried about product quality? Our quality control and inspection services ensure that the products you receive meet your standards. We conduct thorough inspections and provide detailed reports, giving you peace of mind.

Logistics and Shipping

Shipping products from China can be complex. Our logistics and shipping services take care of everything from packaging to delivery. We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your products arrive safely and on time.

After-Sales Support

Our commitment to you doesn't end with the purchase. We offer after-sales support to help you resolve any issues that may arise with your orders. We're here to assist you with returns, refunds, and any other concerns.

Consultation Services

Looking to expand your business in China? Our consultation services provide expert advice on market trends, business strategies, and regulatory compliance. We help you make informed decisions to grow your business.
